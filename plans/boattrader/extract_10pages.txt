BoatTrader Private Seller Extraction — 10 Pages

Go to https://www.boattrader.com/boats/by-owner/ to see private seller listings.

For each listing on the page:
- Click the boat listing title text below a photo
- Read the URL from the browser address bar
- Scroll down to the Description section
- Read Year, Make, Model, Price, Phone, Seller from the page
- Go back to the search results
- Process next listing

After all listings on a page, click Next to go to next page.
Process 10 pages, up to 100 listings total.
