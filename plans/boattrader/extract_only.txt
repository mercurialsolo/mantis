BoatTrader Private Seller Lead Extraction

Search BoatTrader for private seller boats near Miami:
- Go to https://www.boattrader.com/boats/
- Apply filters in this order (MOST IMPORTANT FIRST):
  1. Select "Private Seller" in seller type filter
  2. Enter zip code 33101 in location field
  3. Set radius to 25 miles
  4. Set minimum price to $35,000
  5. Sort by "Recently Updated" newest first
- After applying filters, VERIFY:
  - The page title or heading should mention "by owner" or "private"
  - The result count should be less than 5,000
  - If filters were lost (shows 125,000+ boats), re-apply starting from Private Seller
- CRITICAL: Do NOT click condition pills (All/New/Used) — they change the page URL and lose other filters

For each listing in the search results:
- Click the listing TITLE TEXT (not the photo) to open the detail page
- IMMEDIATELY read the URL from the browser address bar — this is mandatory
- Scroll DOWN aggressively — at least 5-6 times past the photo gallery
- Phone numbers are in the Description or Seller Notes section, which is BELOW the photos
- Do NOT click "Contact Seller" or "Request Info" buttons — they open popup traps
- Look for phone: (305)555-1234, 786-555-1234, 10+ digit numbers
- NOT phones: prices, years, zip codes, model numbers
- Extract ALL of: Year, Make, Model, Price, URL, Phone (or "none"), Seller Name
- A listing is VIABLE even without a phone — but phone is the highest value field
- Go back to results (Alt+Left)
- Process all listings on the page, then click Next page
- Continue until no more pages

Scale:
- Process up to 200 listings across up to 50 pages
- Allow 40 steps per listing extraction (click, scroll deep for phone, read, go back)
- Extract partial data — do not skip listings just because phone is missing
