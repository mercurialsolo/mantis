Update the industry vertical for a qualified lead in StaffAI CRM

URL: https://staffai-test-crm.exe.xyz
Requires login session: yes

Inputs:
  lead_status = "Qualified" (Status filter for selecting the lead)
  industry_value (required) (New industry vertical value)

Steps:
1. Go to the Leads Page
2. Select the first lead with the "{{lead_status}}" status
3. Go to the Edit Lead page for this lead
4. Update the Industry Vertical to "{{industry_value}}"
5. Click "Update Lead"
6. Verify: page should contain "{{industry_value}}"
