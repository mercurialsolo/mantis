Update the industry vertical for a qualified lead in StaffAI CRM

URL: https://staffai-test-crm.exe.xyz
Requires login session: yes

Inputs:
  lead_status = "Qualified" (Status filter for selecting the lead)
  industry_value (required) (New industry vertical value)

Steps:
1. Click on "LEADS"
2. Click on "{{lead_status}}"
3. Click on the first lead row in the table
4. Click on "Edit"
5. Click on the "Industry Vertical" input field
6. Type "{{industry_value}}"
7. Click on "Update Lead"
8. Verify: page should contain "{{industry_value}}"
